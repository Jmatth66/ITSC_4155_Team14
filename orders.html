How it works

For each item you can select the amount you want in:

  • Bags
  • Barrels
  • Sacks
  • Pallets

If you don't want an item in the other containers then you can simply leave the box blank, or type a zero. For example, if I wanted to order 10 bags of Hog feed then I would type 10 in the box next to bags, and leave the other boxes blank.


Weights and Amounts

The weight of each item goes as follows:

Type Weight Amount in Bags
Bag 50 lbs. 1
Barrel 350 lbs. 7
Sack 2,000 lbs. 40
Pallet 2,000 lbs. 40

*Barrels and pallets add additional weight. Roughly 10-15 lbs. This weight does NOT take away from the weight of the grain / feed inside!
